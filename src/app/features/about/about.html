Sree Narayana Group of Institutions is defined by its progressive nursing and allied science curricula. Guided by Guru's teachings, we believe education is the ultimate force for social transformation.

Every simulation suite, clinical lab, and physical therapy center is meticulously curated to challenge students. We forge highly resilient professionals capable of thriving in high-pressure medical environments globally.

Located in Bangalore's Yashavanthapura — just 10 minutes from the railway station — our campus combines modern infrastructure with a spirit of compassion that runs through every program and every faculty member.
